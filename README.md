## 🌍 The Problem

**1.1 billion people worldwide have no legal identity.**

This locks them out of banking, healthcare, employment, and the digital economy. The problem spans three underserved groups:

- **Refugees & stateless people** — fleeing conflict with no documents and no legal standing
- **Informal & gig workers** — skilled professionals with no verifiable record
- **Citizens of developing nations** — excluded from digital infrastructure entirely

Both sectors share the same wound: no verifiable reputation, no payment security, no visibility.

---

## 💡 The Solution

**TrustID** is a self-sovereign identity platform where individuals own, control, and selectively share verifiable credentials — stored on decentralised infrastructure, accessible from anywhere, owned by no one but the user.

- ✅ **No email. No government ID. No central authority.**
- 🔒 **Credentials encrypted by Lit Protocol** — you control who sees what
- 📦 **Stored permanently on Storacha** — censorship-resistant and tamper-proof
- 🔑 **Wallet-based onboarding** — your crypto wallet is your identity anchor

## ⚙️ How It Works

```
User connects wallet
        ↓
Uploads credential (PDF / image / document)
        ↓
Lit Protocol encrypts the file
        ↓
Storacha stores it permanently (content-addressed)
        ↓
User grants access to a specific wallet address
        ↓
Verifier decrypts only what was shared — nothing more
```
